Form factor v4 — smart cap + passive donut

Hub (base + central column) carries display + buttons + MCU. Donut is a passive open-top ring (no flaps). Smart cap sits over the donut, with a single hinged door at the 6 o'clock active position; cap underside holds camera, illumination + status LEDs. Cap-to-hub via 6 pogo contacts at the rim.

Exploded section view — hub / donut / cap

Refill flow: lift cap off → lift donut off → fill all 7 open bays on counter → drop donut back into hub groove → cap back on (magnetic alignment seats pogo contacts).
